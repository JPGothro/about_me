You know it's all about John

As you should have realized, John is the center of the universe. Or as he calls it, the John-verse. Since you are in his orbit, it would make sense to know something about him. Darkness and cruelty await those who don't.

It actually says that in the little book that comes with it: the most popular gun in American crime. Like they're actually proud of that shit.
