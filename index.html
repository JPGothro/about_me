You know it's all about John

As you should have realized, John is the center of the universe. Or as he calls it, the John-verse. Since you are in his orbit, it would make sense to know something about him. Darkness and cruelty await those who don't.

In case you think you know...

Here are some questions to test your actual knowledge of all things John:

  1. Was John born in the Pacific Northwest?
  2. Does John have a valid passport?
  3. Has John ever had a broken bone?
  4. Has he had any viable off-spring?
  5. Is John a friend-of-dogs?
